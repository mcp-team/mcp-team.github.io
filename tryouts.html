ICPC Tryouts 2021

Sponsored by Intel

Event Details

Open to all McMaster students. Take part in ICPC Tryouts for the chance to win prizes and the right to represent McMaster at the ICPC ECNA programming competition! Participants will complete a HackerRank programming contest and the top 15 eligible students will qualify for regionals. Note that students will be given the option to share their score, resume and contact information with Intel.

Important Notes:
- This is an individual contest
- Online materials may not be accessed during the contest
- Prizes will be given to the top three participants
- Students will be given the option to share their score, resume and contact information with Intel

Prizes:
- First Place gets $100
- Second Place gets $75
- Third Place gets $50
- T-shirt for everyone in top 15

Location: Online
Date: February 13th, 2021
Time: 11:00AM - 2:30pm
