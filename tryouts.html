ICPC Tryouts 2024

Event Details

Open to all McMaster students. Take part in ICPC Tryouts for the chance to win prizes and the right to represent McMaster at the ICPC ECNA programming competition! Participants will complete a HackerRank programming contest and the top 9 eligible students will qualify for regionals. Note that students will be given the option to share their score, resume and contact information with Intel.

Important Notes:
- This is an individual contest, collaboration is not permitted
- Use of online resources such as programming language documentation is allowed
