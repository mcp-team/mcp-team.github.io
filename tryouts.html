MCP Tryouts

Sponsored by Intel

Event Details

McMaster Tryouts for the International Collegiate Programming Competition (ICPC)! ICPC is a worldwide programming competition with more than 50 000 students participating.

These tryouts determine who McMaster will send to compete in regionals. At regionals students will compete in teams of three. The top fifteen participants in these tryouts will form our five teams this year.

Important Notes:
- This is an individual contest
- Online materials may not be accessed during the contest
- Prizes will be given to the top three participants
- Students will be given the option to share their score, resume and contact information with Intel

Location: Online
Date: February 11th 2021
Time: 6:45pm - 10:00pm
